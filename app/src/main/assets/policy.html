We will provide you with faster and more convenient service. Therefore, we need your authorization for the following data. As a loan platform, we will analyze your application data for risk assessment and recommend suitable loan products to you. Your data will never be leaked to any third party.


SMS INFORMATION
We collect and store personal messages in your inbox.
We collect messages sent by senders. By analyzing your SMS and various accounts, transaction amount and transaction date, we can determine your risk status, and then provide you with appropriate credit analysis. This enables you to obtain financial facilities from regulated financial entities and other service providers available on the platform. In view of the current imperfect online credit system, all your messages are an important basis for our approval. We will only collect SMS information with your explicit consent. SMS will be uploaded to https://api.quamte.com/. After we determine your risk status, we will delete the SMS.


CONTACT LIST INFORMATION
As part of our loan process, we need proof of the loan applicant. In this regard, in the process of filling out the form on our application, we will collect your contact information to detect close contacts including name and phone numbers, so that you can automatically fill in the data. This can also help us detect credible references. The more credible the reference, the lower the risk associated with users. We collect contact information only after obtaining your additional explicit consent. Contact information will be uploaded to https://api.quamte.com/. After analyzing your risk situation, we will delete your contact information.


CAMERA & IMAGES & PHOTOS INFORMATION
We require your authorization to take photos, which provides you with a smooth experience and enables you to click on photos of your KYC documents and other necessary documents and upload them into the app during your loan application process. These can bring you a smooth use experience and can also prove that you are operating it yourself. This information will be uploaded to https://api.quamte.com/. After determining your risk profile, we will delete the information.


INSTALLED APPS INFORMATION
As an important reference for loan approval, we will collect information about the financial application you installed, including package name, name, installation time, update time, version code, version number, etc. By analyzing this information, we can evaluate your credit value, enrich your personal data through pre-approved customized loans, and recommend appropriate loan products to you. We collect installed apps information only with your express consent. Installed apps information will be uploaded to https://api.quamte.com/. After determining your risk profile, we will delete your installed apps information.


Location
When you apply for a loan, we collect the real location information of your device to prevent fraud risks. We also need your location information to ensure you are within our service area.


Device Information
We collect some specific information about your device for anti-fraud purposes, including Google Advertising ID phone brand, manufacturer and Android version.


STORAGE
We require storage permission so that your KYC and other relevant documents can be securely downloaded and saved on your phone. You can then easily upload the correct KYC related documents for faster loan application details filling and disbursal process. This ensures that you are provided with a seamless experience while using the application.
